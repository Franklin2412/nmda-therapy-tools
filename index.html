About NMDA Encephalitis

Autoimmune NMDA receptor encephalitis is a serious neurological condition that can cause cognitive, speech, behavioral, and motor challenges in children. Recovery often requires intensive, prolonged rehabilitation. These tools are designed to complement professional therapy and support practice at home.
